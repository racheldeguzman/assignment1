Macaroni and Cheese:

TIME | 15-20 MIN

Items needed:

  • Box of uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups of the cheese of your choice (cheddar preferably)
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • Optional: paprika, chili, spice of your choice

Cooking Directions:

  1. Boil macaroni according to package directions
  2. Drain macaroni using strainer
  3. In separate saucepan (medium heat), melt butter
  4. Stir and add milk slowly
  5. At low heat, stir in cheeses at low heat-- until the sauce is thick/melted
  6. Put drained macaroni in pot and add cheese creation
  7. Stir and add chili/spice if desired
